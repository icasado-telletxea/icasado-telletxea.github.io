About me

Currently I am a PhD student in machine learning at the Basque Center for Applied Mathematics (BCAM) in Bilbao, Basque Country (Spain). I am happy to announce that starting next semester I will be a Research Associate (postdoc) at the Manchester Centre for AI Fundamentals at the University of Manchester.

My research interests lie at the intersection of machine learning and mathematics, with a focus on learning theory, generalization bounds, and Bayesian methods.
